About me

I'm a UI UX Designer and a Front-end developer. I've been fascinated at how computers work, the speed, the accuracy, the beautiful designs created and beauty of creations, all this was a major pointer that drove me into the designs world. I have a huge interest in creating things that work and designs that are useful and fast and accurate. Learning Graphic Design was more like an avenue to realizing my goal.
